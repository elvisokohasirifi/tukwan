About us

The name behind travel and tour

We are a Tourism Operations company and we focus on inland tourism both nationals and expatriates. We cater for all your travelling needs and provide you with basic information about the place you are travelling to, including available bus terminals, general prices of items and fees to tour sites.

We also ease your tour by offering you affordable rentals and bookings to make your stay worthwhile.

We are bringing everything closer to your fingers when travelling. When travelling, just go because we will carry your stress while you enjoy. We hope to make Tukwan a global word at the mention of travel or tour.
